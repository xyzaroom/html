About

“Xyzaroom” or Xyzaroom Records, and simply known officially name as Xyzaroom Records Inc. , is an Indonesian record label that subsidiary label and was acquired in 2019 by Algoritma Music Group, Company. Ltd. through the imprint of Algoritma Music Publishing. It was established as the first record label 2018 based in Parungkuda Kota Sukabumi in INDONESIA by Algoritma Records, Algoritma Music Group, and Algoritma Music Publishing.
